Encina Script is a formal calligraphy typeface, carefully designed by Måns Grebäck.
Its swirly, thin strokes organically makes up large and delicate letters. 

Encina Script works perfectly in magazines, invitations and logotypes that require an elegant twist. 
It comes in two versions, with two different sizes of the upper case alphabet, to make it work in all contexts. 

The typeface's contextual alternates makes every word begin and finish in a natural and beautiful way.
